About Me

I believe that data has a story to tell if you're willing to listen. My journey started with a Bachelor's in IT Engineering, where I explored databases, machine learning, and cloud computing. Professionally, I've worked as a Data Engineering Analyst at Accenture, a Data Science Intern at Jio Platforms, and now as a Data Engineer at Adaptive Waves.

Beyond code and data, I enjoy leading teams, organizing events, and volunteering for causes that matter. Whether it's designing AI-powered chatbots, optimizing ETL pipelines, or researching applications of cloud and AI in healthcare, I thrive at the intersection of technology, problem-solving, and impact.

My long-term vision is to continue growing as a data-driven leader, helping organizations re-engineer processes, improve efficiency, and unlock growth with AI and analytics.
